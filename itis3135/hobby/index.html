My Hobby: Guitars


About me

My name is Gabriel Scardo and one of my favorite hobbies is playing guitar. It is not my only hobby, I also play piano as well. My interest in playing guitar started in 2016 and has persisted to this day.

On this site I will show you some background into why I stared playing guitar. The equipment I use and other fun things.

Why I stared playing guitar

One of the main reasons I started playing guitar was inspiration. I remember listening to one of my favorite artists: Joe Satriani. I listened to one of his live shows and was blow away by his guitar playing. From that moment on I wanted to play guitar. All of this was in 2016 and I have been playing guitar ever since

The equipment I use

In total I have probably spent about 3500$ on all of my guitar equipment. It may seem alot but you have to consider this is over a 7 year period. Also I play my guitars almost everyday so I get my value out of them

The main reason I play guitar today is it brings me joy.

Though it isnt always joyful; sometimes it can be extremely frustrating to play. What I mean my this is sometimes you reach a point in playing where you dont seem to get any better. This stagnation has cause me to not want to play. I had to push through the struggle and keep playing. Once you get over the stagnation it's very rewarding to see myself improve
